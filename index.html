Color Grading

The Process

From upload to delivered in four steps.

01

Upload Your Footage

Drop files directly or connect your Google Drive, Dropbox, or Frame.io. Any format accepted.

02

AI Assembles a Cut

Our models analyze pacing, audio levels, and visual quality to produce a structured rough cut automatically.

03

Editors Refine It

A human editor reviews, polishes transitions, grades color, mixes audio, and adds motion elements.

04

You Review & Approve

Comment directly on the timeline. Revisions are turned around within the hour, not days.
